▶ How the score works rating · proximity · review volume

Score = 0.50 × rating + 0.30 × closeness + 0.20 × review score, all on a 0–5 scale.

Review score is review volume log-normalized to 0–5, so a store with thousands of reviews gets credit without drowning out rating quality. Closeness normalizes the driving time from Brooklyn Borough Hall across the scored set — a shorter drive scores higher. The Reviews-idx and Closeness inputs aren't shown as columns, but the Drive time column reflects the same routing.

▶ About the ratings verify before you drive

Ratings and review counts come from the most store-specific public snippet found per location, linked from each row's review count. Newer stores often have few reviews, and some have none yet — those rows stay unscored rather than guessed. Treat everything as a starting point and confirm on Google Maps before a final trip decision.
